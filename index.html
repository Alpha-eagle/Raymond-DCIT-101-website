About Raymond

Raymond Adoe is a level 100 Physical Science student. He has always loved computers
from the time he was born. His dream is to develop softwares that will help improve lives
in his country.
Unfortunate for him he was given Physical Sciences instead of Computer Science
He thought his dreams had come to an end, untill one day he was told by a lecturer that
he was could still offer Computer science since Physical Sciences and Mathematical Sciences
are in the same department. From that day onwards Raymond thrived to be the best in all
the DCIT courses to get him to offer his dream courses and pursue a career he has always dreamt of
And he is still working hard to major computer science untill now.
